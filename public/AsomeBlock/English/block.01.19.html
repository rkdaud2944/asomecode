  • 1. Learning Objective

    • • Understand the principle of a status value
    • Understand 4 status values.
    • Create and execute different actions according to status values.



  • 2. Preview

    • A. Motion detection robots

    Have you seen robots at shopping centers? These robots take care of people and help them get directions. To do that, robots have many sensors and AI programs.


    B. AsomeBot can also recognize hand gestures.

    Although you can't make them as great as robots at airports, AsomeBot can understand and execute commands that we do by hand. Then, you can make AsomeBot do what you want every time you create a program or even without a smartphone!

    To move AsomeBot with your hands, you must first use an ultrasonic sensor. You can check the status of how the distance value measured by the ultrasonic sensor changes and perform the desired actions according to each condition.

    AsomeBot recognizes a total of 4 health values.

    ① The first status is a hand (obstacle) in a distant place (farther than 20 cm and closer than 50 cm) and then further away or disappeared by 50 cm. The status value of the state is saved as '1'. It is mainly used to stop AsomeBot in action.

    ② The second status is a hand that is far away (more than 20 cm and closer than 50 cm) coming closer (within 20 cm). The status value of the state is saved as '2'. At this time, talk about what motion should be performed.

    ③ The third status is a hand that is near (within 20 cm) suddenly disappears or moves rapidly out of 50 cm. The status value of the state is saved as '3'. At this time, talk about what motion should be performed.

    ④ Fourth, when the hand that is near (within 20cm) moves to a distant place within 50cm. The status value of the state is saved as '4'. At this time, talk about what motion should be performed.

  • 4. Play Activity

    • A. Game Rule

    ① Talk about what motion should be created for the status corresponding to 1-4.

    ※ Obstacles in the 1 and 3 statuses already have disappeared. Therefore, refrain from walking motion or motions that repeat too long as AsomeBot may fall off the desk.

    ② Talk about what other status can be created with an ultrasonic sensor.

    ③ Talk about types of devices or programs that would be convenient to use with hand signals.


    B. Algorithm

    How was it making a hand command program? Now, introduce your friends about new functions you created.

    ① Demonstrate hand command motions and share the names of new motions.

    ② Also share why you created the hand command motions.

    ③ Listen to your friends’ hand command story.

    ④ Talk about what other functions you can make besides the motions you created.
